Admissions · 2026/27

Begin your child's Le Poshe journey.

We keep classes intentionally small, which means places are limited and admissions are personal. Every family meets with our proprietress before a place is offered.

— Four Simple Steps

How to apply.

Our process is warm and unhurried. We move at the pace of the family — usually two to four weeks from first enquiry to enrolment.

01

Enquire

Submit the form below or call us. We respond within 24 hours with a brochure and available dates.

02

Visit

Schedule a private tour. Meet the proprietress, walk the school, observe a class in session.

03

Assessment

A short, age-appropriate assessment so we understand your child's readiness and learning style.

04

Welcome

On acceptance, we send a welcome pack and arrange a smooth, gentle first day.

— Requirements

What you'll need to get started.

We keep paperwork to a minimum. The four documents below are all we ask at enquiry stage.

Birth certificate
A copy of your child's birth certificate or an immigration document showing date of birth.
Passport photograph
Two recent passport-sized photographs of the child.
Previous school report (if applicable)
Most recent end-of-term or end-of-year report. Not required for first-time school entrants.
Immunisation record
Standard Nigerian immunisation card or equivalent, up to date for your child's age.

— Common Questions

Things parents always ask.

What are your school fees?
Fees vary by class and are reviewed annually. Please submit the enquiry form above and we will send our current fee schedule and payment options directly to your email. We offer termly and full-year payment plans.
What ages do you accept?
We accept children from age 18 months (crèche) through to age 11 (Primary 6).
Do you offer transportation?
We arrange optional school bus services along established Parkview and Old Ikoyi routes. Please ask during your tour for current routes and capacity.
What is your meal policy?
Hot, freshly-prepared lunches and a healthy snack are provided daily. We accommodate common dietary requirements with advance notice.
Can my child join mid-year?
Yes, subject to available places in the relevant class. We work closely with the family and previous school to ensure a smooth transition.
What secondary schools do your pupils proceed to?
Our graduates have placed at British International School, Greensprings, Day Waterman, Atlantic Hall, Children's International, and many top-tier Lagos secondary schools. We support every family through the secondary application process.
